# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone interacting with the VERTEX community.

Whether you're reporting bugs, suggesting features, or participating in discussions, we ask everyone to contribute in a constructive and respectful manner.

---

## Expected Behavior

We encourage community members to:

* Be respectful and professional.
* Be constructive when providing feedback.
* Help others whenever possible.
* Respect different opinions and experiences.
* Focus discussions on improving the project.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

* Harassment or personal attacks.
* Discrimination of any kind.
* Spam or self-promotion unrelated to the project.
* Sharing malicious content or attempting to disrupt the community.
* Publishing security vulnerabilities without responsible disclosure.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please contact the VERTEX team privately.

Reports will be handled confidentially and reviewed fairly.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, discussions, or community members whose behavior negatively impacts the project or its community.

---

## Our Goal

VERTEX is more than software.

We're building a community around reliability, collaboration, and continuous improvement, and we want everyone to feel welcome while contributing to that vision.
